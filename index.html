👋 who am i

hey, i'm Abhinav Verma — a software engineer from Lucknow, now living my best life in Bangalore. i build things at the intersection of AI and production systems. not just the "look ma, i used GPT" kind — i mean actual agentic pipelines, LLM routers, RAG systems with real latency wins.

🛠️ what i actually do

currently building an AI-powered newsletter platform at Astilbe Page — full-stack (React + Node.js + MongoDB), LLM routing with Mistral-7B, RBAC, email pipelines, the works. before that, i was at Tektronix automating hardware testing with Python and SCPI, and at Astilbe Page (Pune), building compliance-focused AI agents. graduated from RVCE Bangalore with a degree in Electrical & Electronics Engineering — that's where the chaos began.

🧠 the tech obsession

i care deeply about systems that aren't just clever — they have to be reliable, observable, and maintainable. a RAG pipeline that shaved 30% latency? satisfying. a clean React UI with proper RBAC? equally satisfying. i've got 400+ DSA problems solved on LeetCode, which is either impressive or a cry for help, honestly.

🎭 the plot twist — i do theatre

yeah, the AI engineer writes and performs street plays. i've done multiple productions across Bangalore and national-level events with CARV Hindi Theatre. theatre taught me storytelling, improvisation, and how to hold a room — skills that surprisingly transfer to technical presentations and team collaboration.

📐 giving back

i mentor underprivileged students in mathematics. accessible education is something i believe in genuinely — not just as a tagline. grew up in Lucknow, studied in Kanpur, grinded through Bangalore — i know what it means to figure things out without a playbook.

☕ when i'm not shipping

exploring Bangalore's ever-growing food scene, rehearsing for the next play, or deep in a DSA rabbit hole at 2am. occasionally touch grass. sometimes in that order.
